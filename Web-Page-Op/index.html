Luffy Punching a Celestial Dragon

Luffy's punch on the Celestial Dragon is one of the most iconic moments in One Piece. It showcases his defiance of authority and his unwavering sense of justice. This act not only shocked the world but also solidified Luffy's character as someone who stands up for what's right, no matter the consequences.
